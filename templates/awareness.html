Skin Disease Awareness

Why is Skin Health Important?

Your skin is your body's largest organ and serves as a protective barrier against the environment. It's crucial to maintain good skin health and be aware of any changes that might indicate a potential problem.

Common Signs to Watch For

  • New moles or changes in existing moles
  • Persistent rashes or itchy patches
  • Unusual lumps or growths
  • Changes in skin color or texture
  • Non-healing sores or wounds

The ABCDEs of Melanoma

When examining moles, remember the ABCDE rule:

  • Asymmetry: One half doesn't match the other
  • Border: Irregular, ragged, or blurred edges
  • Color: Variety of colors or uneven distribution of color
  • Diameter: Larger than 6mm (about the size of a pencil eraser)
  • Evolving: Changes in size, shape, or color over time

Prevention Tips

  1. Use sunscreen daily (SPF 30 or higher)
  2. Avoid tanning beds
  3. Wear protective clothing when outdoors
  4. Perform regular skin self-examinations
  5. Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy diet
Remember: Early detection is key in treating skin diseases effectively. If you notice any unusual changes in your skin, consult a dermatologist promptly.

While DermaAI can help identify potential skin conditions, it's not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.
