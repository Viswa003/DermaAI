About DermaAI

DermaAI is an advanced skin disease prediction tool that uses artificial intelligence to analyze skin images and identify potential skin conditions. Our goal is to provide a quick and easy way for users to get an initial assessment of their skin concerns.

How It Works

  1. Upload an image of the skin area you're concerned about.
  2. Our AI model analyzes the image and compares it to a vast database of skin conditions.
  3. The system provides a prediction of the most likely skin condition, along with a confidence score.
Important: DermaAI is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

Supported Conditions

Our current model can detect the following skin conditions:

  • Actinic Keratoses (akiec)
  • Basal Cell Carcinoma (bcc)
  • Benign Keratosis-like Lesions (bkl)
  • Dermatofibroma (df)
  • Melanoma (mel)
  • Melanocytic Nevi (nv)
  • Vascular Lesions (vasc)

We are continuously working to improve our model and expand the range of detectable conditions.
